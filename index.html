History Los Angeles Clippers

The Clippers started as the Buffalo Braves before moving to San Diego from 1978 to 1984. The Clippers played at the Los Angeles Memorial Sports Arena from 1984 to 1999 before joining the Lakers and the NHL's Kings at Staples Center. (The WNBA's Sparks have also played here since 2001.) The 2008-09 season marked the Clippers' 25th in Los Angeles. In 2012, the arena hosted three playoff teams -- the Kings, Clippers and Lakers -- for the first time.
